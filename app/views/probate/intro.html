About this prototype

This prototype is based on requirements captured from interviews with applicants and business needs from the probate registries.

It is the first step to create a simpler, streamlined online probate application process.

What this prototype covers

This covers a simple personal application, where:

  • the probate applicant is one of two executors named in his mother’s will, the other executor is not applying
  • the applicant has the death certificate and the original will, there are no codicils
  • his mother lived in England and was a widow
  • no inheritance tax was payable, he completed the correct forms online and he received an IHT identifier code

For the purpose of testing this prototype please answer the questions as if you are the person above.

What it doesn’t cover

This prototype doesn't cover more complex applications. If you select an area that is not covered yet you will see an error page - from there you will be able to continue.
