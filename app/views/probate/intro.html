About this prototype

This prototype is based on requirements captured from interviews with applicants and business needs from the probate registries.

It is the first step to create a simpler, streamlined online probate application process.

What this prototype covers

This covers a simple personal application where:

  • the applicant is one of two executors named in his mother’s will, the other executor is not applying
  • he has the death certificate and the original will, there are no codicils
  • his mother resided in England and was a widow
  • no inheritance tax was payable, the correct forms were completed online and he received an IHT identifier code.

For the purpose of testing this prototype please complete the form as if you are the personal applicant above.

What it doesn’t cover

More complex applications are not yet covered by this prototype. Areas that are not covered yet are greyed out, and will be added at a later date.
